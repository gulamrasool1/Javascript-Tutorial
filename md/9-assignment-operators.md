# assignment operator
[JS FILE](../js/9-assignmentOperators.js)
>table of assignment operator

|operator|example|same as|
|--------|-------|-------|
|=|x=y|x=y|
|+=|x+=y|x=x+y|
|-=|x-=y|x=x-y|
|*=|x*=y|x=x*y|
|/=|x/=y|x=x/y|
|%=|x%=y|x=x%y|
|**=|x**=y|x=x**y|
